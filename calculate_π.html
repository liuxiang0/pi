2π=22⋅2+22⋅2+2+22⋯\displaystyle {\frac {2}{\pi }}={\frac {\sqrt {2}}{2}}\cdot {\frac {\sqrt {2+{\sqrt {2}}}}{2}}\cdot {\frac {\sqrt {2+{\sqrt {2+{\sqrt {2}}}}}}{2}}\cdotsπ2​=22​​⋅22+2​​​⋅22+2+2​​​​⋯
